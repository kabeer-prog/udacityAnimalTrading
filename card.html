Panda

The giant panda lives in a few mountain ranges in central China, mainly in Sichuan, but also in neighbouring Shaanxi and Gansu.

  • Scientific Name: A. melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 1.9m
  • Average Lifespan: 20years
  • Habitat: Snowy and Rocky Habitat

It is characterised by its bold black-and-white coat and rotund body. The name "giant panda" is sometimes used to distinguish it from the red panda, a neighboring musteloid....
